About Me

I'm a machine learning engineer with 8y+ experience in the industry designing & building intelligent systems at scale. I've worked cross-functionally with product, engineering, research and data science teams on various projects spanning machine learning, natural language understanding and software development domains.

I like to think about products holistically and believe that finding the right balance between quality (research), performance (engineering), complexity (product) and ease of use (UX) is the key to building good products that delight users around the world.

I am passionate about making confusing data science concepts understandable for beginners. My articles on medium.com (https://medium.com/@ayush2991) aim to explain confusing concepts using simple and highly intuitive examples.
