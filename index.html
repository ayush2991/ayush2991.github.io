About Me

I am a Machine Learning Engineer at Google, passionate about building intelligent systems that push the boundaries of technology. My expertise spans various domains, including deep learning, natural language processing, and the exciting field of Agentic AI.

Beyond core machine learning, I have a keen interest in quantitative finance, exploring the intersection of advanced algorithms and financial markets. I'm also fascinated by the fundamental principles of quantum physics and its potential implications for computation and beyond.

I believe in continuous learning and enjoy tackling complex problems. This portfolio showcases some of my work and interests.
