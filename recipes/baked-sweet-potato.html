Baked Sweet Potato Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Wash and scrub the sweet potatoes thoroughly.
  3. Pat them dry and pierce each sweet potato several times with a fork.
  4. Rub the sweet potatoes with olive oil and sprinkle with salt, pepper, and cinnamon if using.
  5. Place the sweet potatoes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-60 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender and can be easily pierced with a fork.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before serving.
