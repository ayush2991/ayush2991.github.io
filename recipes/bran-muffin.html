Healthy Bran Muffins Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup wheat bran
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a muffin tin or line with muffin liners.
  2. In a large bowl, mix wheat bran and buttermilk. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
  3. In another bowl, beat together oil, egg, sugar, and vanilla. Add to the bran mixture and stir well.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Stir into the bran mixture until just blended. Fold in raisins if using.
  5. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about 2/3 full.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
  7. Allow muffins to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
