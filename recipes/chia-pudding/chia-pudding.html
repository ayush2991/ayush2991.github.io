Chia Pudding Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup (or honey)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Fresh fruits and nuts for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine chia seeds, almond milk, maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  2. Stir well to combine.
  3. Let the mixture sit for about 5 minutes, then stir again to prevent clumping.
  4. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  5. Before serving, stir the pudding again and top with fresh fruits and nuts if desired.
