Indian Style Instant Pot Dal

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Toor Dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 1/2 cup Moong Dal (split yellow lentils)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the Toor Dal and Moong Dal thoroughly under running water.
  2. Turn on the Instant Pot and set it to 'Sauté' mode. Add oil.
  3. Once the oil is hot, add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  4. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes, green chilies, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes turn soft.
  7. Add the rinsed dals and mix well.
  8. Add 3 cups of water and stir well.
  9. Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set it to 'Pressure Cook' mode for 8 minutes.
  10. Once the cooking is done, let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then manually release the remaining pressure.
  11. Open the lid and stir the dal. Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed.
  12. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or roti.
